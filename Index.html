EXIF Data & Privacy Guide

What is EXIF Data?

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) is invisible information stored inside image files, automatically recorded by cameras and phones.

  • Technical Details: Camera model, lens type, shutter speed, aperture, and ISO.
  • Location Data: Precise GPS coordinates showing where the photo was captured (if enabled).

Privacy Risks

  1. Location Exposure: GPS data can pinpoint your location.
  2. Device Fingerprinting: Camera model and serial numbers can link photos back to the same device.
